How it works

Corji leverages a number of hither-to underutilized protocols and content discovery mechanisms 4 in order to seed our initial database 6.

The actual SMS heavy lifting is handled by Twilio, with the TwiML being served Flask on EC2 hosts.

Assets are served via S3 + CloudFront.

No corgis were harmed in the process of the development of corgis. 5
